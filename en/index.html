FAQ

Short and clear

Do I need to provide a photo?+

Not required. If you have a photo of your shop or product — great. If not, we can work without one by arrangement.

What format will I receive?+

Usually 9:16 (Reels/Ads). We can also do 1:1 or 16:9 on request.

Can I get an ad made from my own clips?+

Yes. You send your clips, we edit the ad with pacing, subtitles, effects and branding.

What is on-location filming?+

We come to your location and shoot real footage (no AI) which we use to create ads and clips.

How much does on-location filming cost?+

A flat rate of ~€30 — around 1 hour of filming + 3 formats: ad, feed and documentary clip.

How much does a landing page cost?+

~€50 per landing page — modern, mobile-friendly and ready for 2026.

How is the AI ad price calculated?+

~€1 per second of the final video. Example: 15 seconds = ~€15 · 30 seconds = ~€30.

Not sure what I need?+

Send your profile link + goal (more messages, calls or visits) and we'll suggest the most effective format for you.
