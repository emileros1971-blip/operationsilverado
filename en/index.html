FAQ

Short and clear

Do I need a photo?+

Not required. If you have one photo of your shop or product — great. If not, we can work without it.

What format do I get?+

Usually 9:16 (Reels/Ads). On request we also deliver 1:1 or 16:9.

Can I get an ad from my own clips?+

Yes. Send clips from your phone — we edit the ad with pacing, captions, effects and branding.

What is on-location filming?+

We come to you and film real footage (no AI) that we use for ads and clips.

How much does on-location filming cost?+

Flat 3.500 din — oko 1 sat snimanja + 3 formata: reklamni, feed i doku klip.

How much does a website or webshop cost?+

Cena se dogovara based on scope and features — javite se za personalizovanu ponudu.

How is the AI ad price calculated?+

120 din per second of final video. Primer: 15 sekundi = 1.800 din · 30 sekundi = 3.600 din.

Not sure what you need?+

Send your profile link + goal (more messages, calls or visits) and I'll suggest the most effective format for you.
