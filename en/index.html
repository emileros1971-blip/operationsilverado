Frequently Asked Questions

Short and clear

Is a photo required?+

Not at all. If you have one photo of your shop or product — great. If not, we can work without it by agreement.

What format do I get?+

Most commonly 9:16 (Reels/Ads). We also do 1:1 or 16:9 on request.

Can I get an ad made from my own footage?+

Yes. Send your clips from your phone and we'll edit an ad with pace, subtitles, effects and branding.

What is on-location filming?+

We come to your location and film real footage (no AI) which we use for ads and clips.

How much does on-location filming cost?+

A flat $35 — about 1 hour of filming + 3 formats: ad, feed and documentary clip.

How much does a landing page cost?+

Starting from $60 — modern, mobile-ready, built for 2026. Domain and hosting for one year are included.

How is the AI ad price calculated?+

$1.20 per second of the finished video. Example: 15 seconds = $18 · 30 seconds = $36.

Not sure what you need?+

Send your profile link + goal (more messages, calls or visits) and we'll suggest the most effective format for you.
